About T6 Hackathon

The T6 Hackathon is a pioneering event that unites the brightest Muslim programmers, scientists, and designers from the world's most renowned universities—Harvard, Oxford, Cambridge, Stanford, Imperial and MIT. This unique gathering is more than just a tech competition; it's a space where powerful, lasting connections are forged to develop cutting-edge technologies that address global challenges while creating meaningful solutions tailored to the needs of the Ummah. The T6 Hackathon is shaping the future of tech and empowering our community for generations to come, insha'Allah.

FAQ

Does it cost anything to participate?

No, it is completely free. We are currently fundraising to be able to afford flights and accommodation for those selected to compete. If not, the event will be held remotely.

Can I participate solo?

Yes, you can participate solo if you prefer. However, we encourage all applicants to select the team-matching option, which will place you in a group where each member is from a different university. This can be a great way to build connections and enrich your experience.

Can I join the committee?

We are currently recruiting committee members! Please keep an eye on this website for updates, or reach out to Malik Ahmed on LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/malik-ahmed-384470106.

Where will the hackathon be hosted?

The T6 Hackathon will be hosted at Stanford University, with options for remote participation if necessary.

What skills are required to participate?

We welcome participants with a range of skills, including programming, design, and scientific research. Whilst evidence of innovation and creativity is considered, we value enthusiasm, a willingness to learn.

Is the event segregated?

Yes, T6 Hackathon will have a seperate track for brothers and sisters. We will be doing our best to arrange the event in a way which is most pleasing to Allah e.g. allotted prayer times, no music, no free-mixing etc. However, marriage is half of our deen, and we understand this could be a means by which to meet a potential spouse. So if it is requested by enough people, we will endevour to arrange a halal match-making service during the event.
