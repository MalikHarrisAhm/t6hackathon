FAQ

Does it cost anything to participate?

No, it is completely free.

What's the prize???

Insha'Allah, beyond the incredible connections and friends you will make along the way, we are currently arranging a cash prize and an intership at a notable startup for the winners. More details to come.

Can I participate solo?

Yes, you can participate solo if you prefer. However, we encourage all applicants to select the team-matching option, which will place you in a group where each member is from a different university. This can be a great way to build connections and enrich your experience.

Can I join the committee?

We are currently recruiting committee members! Please keep an eye on this website for updates, or reach out to Malik Ahmed on LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/malik-ahmed-384470106.

Where will the hackathon be hosted?

The T6 Hackathon will be most likely be hosted remotely, however, we encourage teams to try and meet up in person where possible.

What skills are required to participate?

We welcome participants with a range of skills, including programming, design, and scientific research. Whilst evidence of innovation and creativity is considered, we value enthusiasm, a willingness to learn.
