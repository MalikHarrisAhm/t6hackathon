T6 Hackathon: Innovating for the Ummah

Where Brilliant Muslim Minds Unite to Shape Our Future

Empowering Excellence, Inspiring Change

T6 Hackathon is a groundbreaking initiative that brings together the most talented Muslim programmers, scientists, designers, and innovators from the world's top six universities: Imperial, Oxford, Cambridge, Stanford, Harvard, and MIT. This extraordinary gathering is more than just a tech event; it's a crucible of innovation where diverse minds from across STEM and creative disciplines converge to forge connections, exchange ideas, and create cutting-edge solutions. Our mission is clear: to harness this collective genius to develop technologies and designs that will not only advance the global tech landscape but also address the unique challenges and opportunities within the Ummah. By fostering collaboration between these exceptional individuals - from coders crafting elegant algorithms to scientists pushing the boundaries of knowledge, and designers shaping intuitive user experiences - T6 Hackathon aims to catalyze a new wave of innovation that will propel our community forward and make a lasting impact on the world. Join us in this exciting journey as we ideate, create, and innovate for a brighter, more connected future for all.
